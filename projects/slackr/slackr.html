Description

I developed an Android app aimed at facilitating online connections among college students through class-based group chats. The app leveraged Google Firebase for user authentication and created a social media-like environment within the application.

I implemented the project using Kotlin and am currently in the process of rewriting it in React Native to ensure cross-compatibility across platforms.
